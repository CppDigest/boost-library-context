> I see most other boost packages are available at version 1.89. What is the plan for spirit to release the same?  
  
Boost 1.89 was released on August 6, 2025 and it includes the latest `master` branch of Spirit. However we didn't have enough human resources and forgot to merge `develop` for the last release.  
  
I became the maintainer of Spirit since May 2025. While the other maintainers are still available, I'm the most active maintainer now. I plan to merge `develop` into `master` as soon as my modernization overhaul boostorg/spirit_x4#4 is complete, but that might take some time.  
  
Meanwhile, you may configure your application to fetch the `develop` branch of Spirit. As long as you target the commit with the green checkmark ("tests passing" status), you'll be safe to do so.

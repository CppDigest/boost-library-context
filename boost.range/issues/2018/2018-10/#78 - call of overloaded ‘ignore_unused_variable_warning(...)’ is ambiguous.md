Hello dear team of Boostorg/range.  
  
I was already at boostorg/geometry, but now the the error continued to exist, and is triggered by `boost/range/concepts.hpp:19`.   
  
The error is pretty much the same as in the closed issue [#9438](https://svn.boost.org/trac10/ticket/9438#no1), because there are ambigous declarations for  `ignore_unused_variable_warning(...)` in `boost` and `lemon`.   
  
In `boostorg/geometry` the method `ignore_unused_variable_warning(...)` was replaced, [this way in #514](https://github.com/boostorg/geometry/pull/514).  
  
- OS: Ubuntu 18.04 Bionic Beaver  
- clang: 6.0  
- lemon: 1.3.1  
- boost: happened under 1.63 and  was reproduced with boost 1.68  
  
```c++  
#include <boost/geometry.hpp>  
#include <lemon/list_graph.h>  
  
namespace bg = boost::geometry;  
namespace bgi = boost::geometry::index;

	Point two = Point(10, 51);

	Node dummy1 = graph.addNode();
```  

**Request:**  
Create a comprehensive Power BI dashboard with 9 pages focused on humanitarian program monitoring. Each page should include relevant visuals, KPIs, and filters. Add interactive maps to the Health & Nutrition, WASH, and FSL pages to show geographic distribution of indicators.  
  
**Required Pages & Features:**  
  
1. **Overview** – Summary of key indicators across all sectors.  
2. **Health & Nutrition** – KPIs (screened, SAM/MAM identified, admissions, recoveries), trends, and a map visualizing health facility performance and nutrition rates by region.  
3. **WASH** – Visuals for access to clean water, sanitation coverage, hygiene promotion activities, with a map of WASH infrastructure by location.  
4. **FSL (Food Security & Livelihoods)** – Food distribution, livelihood support, and a map illustrating food insecurity hotspots and program reach.  
5. **Protection** – GBV cases, child protection activities, and referral pathways.  
6. **Education** – School attendance, learning outcomes, and education support programs.  
7. **Shelter/NFI** – Distribution of shelter kits, NFI coverage, and shelter needs analysis.  
8. **Monitoring & Evaluation** – Indicator tracking, baseline vs. current status, and data quality checks.  
9. **Admin & Logistics** – Supply chain status, stock levels, and facility assessments.  
  
**Additional Requirements:**  
- Include slicers for time (month/year), region, and program type on all pages.  
- Use a clean, intuitive layout with consistent color coding per sector.  
- Dashboard should be designed so it can connect to a real data source when available.  
  
This dashboard will support program monitoring, reporting, and decision-making across humanitarian sectors.

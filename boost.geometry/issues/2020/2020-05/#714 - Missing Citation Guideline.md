Include a bibtex reference item in your readme, wiki, or as a separate file.

Boost.geometry has already been cited a couple of times, but [these citations are inconsistent](https://scholar.google.de/scholar?hl=en&as_sdt=0%2C5&q=Boost.Geometry).
